About Our Church

NJC Belgium is a welcoming church family committed to spiritual growth, practical discipleship, and loving our city through service and outreach.

Our Mission

We exist to help people know Jesus, grow in faith, and live out their calling. Through teaching, prayer, and authentic relationships, we empower believers to impact their world.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church in Belgium where every generation is equipped to worship deeply, serve boldly, and share the Gospel with compassion.

Our Journey

  • 2016: NJC Belgium started as a small prayer group gathering in homes.
  • 2019: Weekly public worship services were launched in Antwerp.
  • 2023: Expanded with youth discipleship, family support, and local outreach.
  • Today: A multicultural church family serving people across Belgium.

What We Believe

Jesus is Lord, the Bible is truth, and the Church is called to love God and people.

How We Worship

Passionate worship, prayer ministry, and Bible-based preaching shape every service.

How We Serve

We support families, students, and newcomers through practical care and relational ministry.
